About BrightSmile Dental

Compassionate care for your entire family

Our Story

Founded in 2010, BrightSmile Dental Clinic has been serving the Gaborone community with exceptional dental care. What began as a small practice with one dentist has grown into a state-of-the-art facility with a team of specialists.

We believe everyone deserves access to quality dental care in a comfortable, judgment-free environment. Our patient-centered approach means we take the time to listen to your concerns and explain all treatment options.
