Dental Implants: What to Expect

Posted on April 28, 2023 by Dr. Kagiso Williams

A complete guide to the dental implant process from consultation to final restoration.

What Are Dental Implants?

Dental implants are artificial tooth roots that provide a permanent base for fixed, replacement teeth. They are made of titanium and are surgically placed into the jawbone.

The Dental Implant Process

Hereâ€™s what you can expect during the dental implant process:

  1. Initial Consultation: We will assess your oral health and determine if you are a candidate for implants.
  2. Implant Placement: The implant is surgically placed into the jawbone.
  3. Healing Period: The implant will need time to integrate with the bone (osseointegration).
  4. Crown Placement: Once healed, a custom crown is placed on the implant.

Benefits of Dental Implants

Dental implants offer several advantages:

  • Natural appearance and function.
  • Improved speech and comfort.
  • Durability and longevity.
  • Preservation of jawbone and facial structure.
